Eradicate Food Waste

According to the USDA, 35% of our food supply is wasted every year. How many times have you thrown good food in the trash simply because you didn't know how to use it in a meal?

Help People Cook

This is the reality: cooking is not always easy. Online, there are thousands if not millions of recipes — but which ones work for you and your kitchen?

While anyone could derive value from PantryPal, we think that young, burgeoning cooks would find it the most helpful. For instance, a college student living in their first apartment likely needs help creating and executing recipes.

Also, we could see families loving this app, as refrigerators for many mouths to feed often get pretty packed and a burst of inspiration from Pantry Pal could prove invaluable!

Before creating my high-fidelity prototype on Figma, I first sketched out basic wireframes to test out different UX approaches.
